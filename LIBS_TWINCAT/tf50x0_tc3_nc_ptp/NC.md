# NC

TwinCAT NC is an assembly of function groups used for the control and regulation of axes or of synchronized axis groups. An NC task consists of one or more channels of type PTP channel, FIFO channel or NCI channel, and their subsidiary parts. Generally, immediately after start-up the NC axes find themselves in one or more PTP channels. Particularly, they are moved to a different channel through a process of reconfiguration then, if necessary.
