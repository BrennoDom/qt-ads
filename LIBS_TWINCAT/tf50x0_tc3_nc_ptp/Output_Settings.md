## Invert Motor Polarity

If set `TRUE`, the parameter Invert Motor Polarity inverts the polarity of the motor and thus inverts the direction of motor rotation.

* `FALSE`: In response to positive drive the axis moves in the direction of larger positions.
* `TRUE`: In response to positive drive the axis moves in the direction of smaller positions.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| 39582655WARNING | |
| Risk of Unexpected Movements  If the encoder counting direction and the motor polarity do not match with each other, the axis will make unexpected movements. | |
