# F\_CONV\_3GROUP\_TO\_2GROUP

![33142240](/tcplclib_tc2_eib/1033/Images/gif/187977739__en-US__Web.gif)

Conversion of a 3-stage group address to a 2-stage group address.

## VAR\_INPUT

```
IN : EIB_GROUP_ADDR;
```

**IN:** 3-stage group address (see [EIB\_GROUP\_ADDR](ms-xhelp:///?Id=beckhoff-b660-4bbb-871b-bcd96d7eaa4e)).
