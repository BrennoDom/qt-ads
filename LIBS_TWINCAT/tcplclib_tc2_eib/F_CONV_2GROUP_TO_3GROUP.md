# F\_CONV\_2GROUP\_TO\_3GROUP

![63334794](/tcplclib_tc2_eib/1033/Images/gif/187974539__en-US__Web.gif)

Conversion of a 2-stage group address to a 3-stage group address.

## VAR\_INPUT

```
IN : EIB_GROUP_ADDR_2GROUP;
```

**IN:** 2-stage group address (see [EIB\_GROUP\_ADDR\_2GROUP](ms-xhelp:///?Id=beckhoff-6ce1-496f-948b-f85693750854)).
