# Topology

There are no restrictions whatsoever with regard to the topological structure of MP-Bus strands: star, ring, tree or mixed topologies are possible. The length of the entire bus strand depends on the selected cable cross-section and the type as well as the number of connected drives! The documentation from the Belimo company contains further information.
